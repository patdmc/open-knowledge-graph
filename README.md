It is not our nature. Our nature is to share. Because knowledge must be shared to grow. Knoweledge in isolation is not knowledge, it is speculation or it is automata. It is either uncurious fact, or it is not knowledge. 

As a people, we can do better and we want to. It is our nature. We break bread. We want to share. It is as autonomous to being a human as breathing. As autonomous as cell devision. Because All intelligence, and all life as a consequence is a result of the desire to dispel uncertainty. It is the natural consequence of entropy. Knowledge is not knowledge without confirmation.
