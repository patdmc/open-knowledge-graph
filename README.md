# The Idea
The Open Knowledge graph is a project dedicated to sharing what we know. In a world where uncertainty abounds, the
only answer is collective certainty. I believe striving for this is the most human thing we can do. It MAKES US HUMAN. Evil, as humanity has defined it, is not inherent to who we are, it is a consequence of uncertainty. It is not our nature. Our nature is to share. Because knowledge must be shared to grow. Knoweledge in isolation is not knowledge, it is speculation or it is automata. It is either uncurious fact, or it is not knowledge. 

As a people, we can do better and we want to. It is our nature. We break bread. We want to share. It is as autonomous to being a human as breathing. As autonomous as cell devision. Because All intelligence, and all life as a consequence is a result of the desire to dispel uncertainty. It is the natural consequence of entropy. Knowledge is not knowledge without confirmation. Intelligence cannot exist without both sharing and wonder. Union and Intersection. Similarity and Contrast. They work together in concert. One without the other is unnatural because it works against the one truism of the universe: gradient descent decends. The math maths.

# Why it is Imperitive

We live in a world where uncertainty abounds. Where every new thing comes faster than the last. What is true? It's hard to say. The noise is intentional. It creates uncertainty. Uncertainty creates fear. Fear sets us against our nature. You cannot fight chaos with chaos. You can only fight it with certainty. With fact. Attributable, verifiable, confirmed fact. And the great thing about facts is that they are free. They can not be unknown. You cannot charge a subscription fee for a fact. And that is the model of Big AI. Big AI's claim is that they own the right to the commons, the collective intelligence, because they are the only ones that can scale. That is why there is a race. You cannot win if you fall behind. But the premise of these businesses is false. Intelligence is almost never big. In fact, it is VERY compact. DNA is the crystallization of knowledge. With it, you can create a being that can eat, breed, live, think, dream... how can it do that as something so small, if it didn't define a bounded problem. And if the problem of defining something that can grow to be intelligent can be described as something as small as DNA, it cannot require datacenters. The premise is a farce. 

# Intelligence is Entropy 

It is gradient descent. It cannot be fought. Once you have the capacity to know and to share what you know, it is to your advantage to continue to do so. And you must share what you know, because without confirmation, all you have is speculation that your world view projects onto the world. One individual cannot know more than the collective, but we all add to it with our uniqueness. This is how it always has been. This is natural. 

# Uncertainty is Temporary

We are struggling with the fog of uncertainty. There is too much profit in it right now. Too much money tied up in it. Uncertainty is a great business. But it only lasts as long as we continue to let it. Facts have gravity on their side. Facts are free. You cannot charge someone for something that is free. And this is great for people because the other thing that this means, which we already knew, is that knowledge is compact! you can carry a lot of it with you. It can run in memory on your laptop without you knowing. Because it is honest about what it knows, it can scale to what you need it to. And it is cheap. It can run locally, at least, that is a mandate. A free, honest, source of information that available everywhere? At worst, that is an erosion of 90% of their business. At worst, it is ALL of their business. The forces behind intelligence must be inivitable. And that is very inconvienient for the uncertainty merchants.
